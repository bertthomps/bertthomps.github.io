Paris

In this mesmerizing city,

tourists come to explore...

...and locals don’t seem to stress,

where discovering old records is a sidewalk away,

art is on every corner,

and music can be found in the most unsuspecting places.

Its citizens always find the time to take a pause

even when there are places to go,

where some live on the edge...

...and others live large.

But whether you like to take in its iconic sights...

...or quietly ponder its deep traditions,

you will always find that there is more to discover in the city of love.
